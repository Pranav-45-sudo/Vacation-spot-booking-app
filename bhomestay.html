Ekanth Stay

Ekanth Forest Homestays, located in the lush forests of Guhagar, Ratnagiri, is a nature lover’s dream. Surrounded by beaches, lakes, mangroves, and heritage temples, this eco-friendly retreat is just a 5-hour drive from Mumbai or Pune.

Chaitanya Niwas

Chaitanya Niwas is a homestay located in the heart of Ratnagiri, Maharashtra. It offers a unique experience of staying in a traditional Maratha house, surrounded by lush greenery and natural beauty. The homestay is perfect for those who want to experience the local culture and lifestyle while enjoying a comfortable and peaceful stay.
